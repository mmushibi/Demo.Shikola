General Rules and Regulations

1. Code of Conduct

Respect: All members must treat others with respect, kindness, and professionalism.

Harassment: Any form of harassment, discrimination, or bullying is strictly prohibited.

Integrity: Members should act honestly and responsibly in all interactions.

2. Attendance and Punctuality

Attendance Policy: Regular attendance is expected; members must notify the school in advance of absences.

Punctuality: Members should arrive on time for classes/meetings.

3. Usage of Facilities

Cleanliness: All members are responsible for keeping the school's premises clean and tidy.

Equipment: Use of equipment must adhere to the guidelines provided; damages should be reported immediately.

4. Safety and Security

Emergency Procedures: Members should familiarize themselves with emergency exits and protocols.

Security: Personal belongings must be secured; the school is not responsible for lost or stolen items.

5. Academic Integrity

Plagiarism: Copying work without proper citation is prohibited.

Exams: Cheating or any form of dishonesty during assessments will lead to severe consequences.

6. Communication

Open Dialogue: Members are encouraged to voice concerns or suggestions respectfully.

Social Media: Conduct on social media reflects on the school; members should be mindful of their online presence.

7. Compliance and Reporting

Policy Adherence: All members must comply with the school's rules and applicable laws.

Reporting Violations: Violations should be reported to the appropriate authority within the school.

Additional Considerations

Disciplinary Actions: Outline potential consequences for violating rules.

Amendments: Procedures for how rules can be amended or updated in the future.
