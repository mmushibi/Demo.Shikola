Optional Subjects (Split Classes) Explained:
  • Regular Subjects: All pupils in the class take the same subject together
  • Optional Subjects: Pupils can choose between different subjects (e.g., Physics vs Chemistry)
  • Groups A & B: Two optional subjects can be scheduled at the same time for different pupil groups
  • Same Time Slot: Group A takes Subject 1 while Group B takes Subject 2 simultaneously
  • Room Assignment: Each optional subject gets its own room and teacher
